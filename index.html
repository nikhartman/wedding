Is a physicist. He also likes cooking, bikes, and dogs.

Steph Rabe

Is a pediatric intensive care nurse. She also likes travel, crafts, and cheese.

Story time.

Steph and Nik met one summer night at half-price happy hour at One World Cafe and quickly became the hottest couple of 2010. Their first few years together were spent attending many ridiculous physics parties, going on epic road trips, and helping each other navigate Baltimore and their degree programs. After first living together in Mount Vernon, they moved to Hampden and the next phase of their relationship, adopting an adorable rescue puppy, Herman. Continuing their journey, they decided to move to British Columbia to give Canadian life a try. Nik, Steph, and little Herman have grown to love the Pacific Northwest; this adventure has brought them even closer together. Nik proposed to Steph in their beautiful kitchen in the Kitsilano neighbourhood of Vancouver, BC. Steph said yes, Herman wagged his tail, and the rest is history. All three are so excited for this next step in life and what the future may hold. They can't wait to celebrate with you at Irvine Nature Center!
